The Three Pillars
Of A Great Smartwatch

We believe in three main things that make a smartwatch great:
Innovative Design and User Interface, Health Tracking, and an App Store

Design
by Studio Shafer

We are working with Studio Shafer to design our watch. We are striving to design a device that is so fashionable, it looks like a high-end, analogue watch.

User Interface
Intuitive and Practical

What's the point of a touch screen on a smartwatch if you can't see what you're touching? Because fingers often cover up much of the valuable screen space when touch screens are used, we are designing an interface without a touchscreen.

Health
More than Steps

With our smartwatch, you will be able to track more than your steps and heart rate. However, for the sake of patenting, we are publicly disclosing what else our watch tracks.

App Store
To make it Yours

We will have a top-notch app store for downloading new, 3rd-party apps to your watch that add functionality that YOU want. You will also be able to download custom, 3rd-party watch faces.
