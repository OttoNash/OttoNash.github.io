Key Features

Designed by Studio Schafer

We are partnering with Studio Schafer to design a modern, fashionable, high-end device that still looks like a classic, analogue watch.

Practical and Intuitive UI

What's the point of a touch screen if you can't see what you're touching? Fingers often cover up much of the valuable smartwatch screen space when touch screens are used. We are designing a User Interface without a touch screen.

Health Beyond Steps and HR

With our smartwatch, you will be able to track more than your steps and heart rate. However, for the sake of patenting, we are not publicly disclosing what else our watch tracks.

An App Store to Make it Yours

We will have a top-notch marketplace for downloading third-party apps and watchfaces to make your watch YOURS. There will also be an application for designing your own watchface.
